Who We Are

An industry-led institute built for high-consequence environments

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute specialising in digital, cyber, and technology risk. We exist to close the critical talent gap in regulated, high-consequence sectors — financial services, critical national infrastructure, and the emerging frontier of AI governance.

Unlike traditional academic programmes, IDR fuses university-grade intellectual rigour with live industry projects, applied frameworks, and employer-connected deployment pathways. Our UK university partnership ensures every programme is evidence-based, while our industry advisory board ensures it is immediately practice-relevant.

Partner With IDR
  • Academic Collaboration UK university partnership ensuring theoretical depth and accreditation pathways for all practitioners.
  • Industry-Led Curriculum Real-world projects, live case studies, and frameworks aligned to NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.
  • AI & Emerging Risk Focus Modules on AI governance, algorithmic risk, model assurance, and the regulatory horizon.
  • Train → Deploy Pipeline A structured pathway from training cohort to employer-ready deployment, not just certification.

Who We Serve

Built for those on the front line of digital risk
